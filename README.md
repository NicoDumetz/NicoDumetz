## AI and computer vision

I work on end-to-end computer vision workflows covering:

* **Image classification** with CNNs, transfer learning, and transformer-based models
* **Object detection** using architectures such as YOLO, RT-DETR, and region-based detection approaches
* **Semantic and instance-aware segmentation** with U-Net, TransUNet, nnU-Net, and mask-based workflows
* Medical image preprocessing, normalization, augmentation, and quality control
* Dataset splitting at the patient or subject level to prevent data leakage
* Model evaluation using metrics such as Dice score, IoU, sensitivity, specificity, precision, recall, F1-score, and AUC
* AI-assisted annotation, prediction correction, and iterative dataset improvement

I am especially interested in the relationship between model architecture, dataset quality, clinical relevance, and real-world generalization.
